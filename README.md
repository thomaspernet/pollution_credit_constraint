
# pollution credit constraint

The Chinese government imposes critical pollution emission objectives during the 11th Five-Year-Plan (2006-2010). The objective of pollution emission was a success and we study one potential mechanism to explain this achievement. One candidate we want to analyse is the role of banks to relocate capital between industry, avoiding financing none environment-friendly project
